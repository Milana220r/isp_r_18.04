Terms of partnership:
To cooperate with the restaurant, you need to contact the representatives of the Public House. Fill out a questionnaire in which you specify:
  • The name of the owner of the company applying for partnership;
  • Contact phone number;
  • Company name;
  • Official Internet resource or links to social media accounts;
  • City and address.
